About Us

Fortfaber Aluminium & GLASS

Fortfaber Aluminium & GLASS remains one of the largest and fastest growing independent, custom manufacturers of aluminium windows and doors in Africa. The Company has been servicing the upper-end residential, light industrial and commercial markets since opening for business in 2006. Fortfaber Aluminium & GLASS operations and services extend throughout Zimbabwe as well as into numerous African countries.
